About us

Who We Are

CodersEdge is providing a platform where anyone can query the combined mobile subscriber databases of all the TSPs in a Licensed service Area(LSA).

What We Do

There is no mechanism with TSP to check the no. of connection working for that individual with other TSPs. So we provide the App and Web application which solve this problem.

  • Any citizen can know the list of mobile connection working against her/his Adhaar number in LSA.
  • DoT can know the list of mobile connection working against a particular Adhaar number in an LSA.
  • The application is also capable of generating the list of mobile connection ,count of which are more than a specific number.
